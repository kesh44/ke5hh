FAQ

A LUT (Look-Up Table) is a mathematical formula that transforms the colors in your video. Think of it as an Instagram filter, but much more precise and professional. When you apply a LUT, it maps each color in your original footage to a new color, creating a specific mood or aesthetic instantly. Our automotive LUTs are specifically designed to enhance car footage with cinematic color grading.
